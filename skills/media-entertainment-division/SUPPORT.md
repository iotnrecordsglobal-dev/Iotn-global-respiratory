## Extended Financial Models

### Detailed Co-Production Financing Scenarios

**Scenario A: Mid-Budget Music Documentary ($8M)**
- Gap Financing: $2.5M (secured against international pre-sales)
- Label Equity: $2M (recoups after debt)
- Streamer MG: $2M (Netflix, 7-year global window)
- Brand Sponsorship: $800K (presenting sponsor + integration)
- Tax Credits: $500K (UK film tax relief)
- Foreign Pre-Sales: $200K (select territories)
- **Total: $8M**

**Scenario B: High-End Concert Film ($12M)**
- Equity Financing: $4M (private equity film fund)
- Label Investment: $3M
- Theatrical Distributor MG: $2.5M (IMAX/premium large format)
- Brand Partnership: $1.5M (tour sponsor extension)
- Tax Incentives: $1M
- **Total: $12M**

**Scenario C: Scripted Biopic ($60M)**
- Major Studio: $45M (80% financing)
- Label Contribution: $7.5M (IP package value)
- International Co-Producer: $5M
- Tax Credits: $2.5M
- **Total: $60M**
- Label retains: 15% equity, soundtrack rights, producer fee, creative consultation
