# Streaming & DSP Relations Consultants

**Primary Use Case:** Develop and execute world-class streaming strategy, DSP relations, and digital distribution frameworks for IOTN Records. This skill integrates the complete strategic playbooks of two industry leaders: **Scott Cohen** and **Darren Hemmings**.

## Scott Cohen: The Platform Label

**Core Philosophy:** The label is a technology-enabled, globally distributed, artist-services platform that must provide transparent, modular, and demonstrable value.

**The Cohen Doctrine:** Every function a label performs must pass this test: "If we were not here, would the artist be worse off?" If the answer is no, that function is dead weight and must be eliminated or transformed.

### Strategy Framework

1. **The Unbundled Label** - Every Service A La Carte
2. **Global Distribution as a Precision Engine**
3. **Data as the New A&R and Marketing Brain**
4. **The Superfan Economy**
5. **The "New Middle Class" of Artists**
6. **The Label as a Venture Investor**
7. **The "Label as an App Store" Concept**
8. **Radical Transparency and Trust**
9. **Organizational Transformation**
10. **The Sustainable Artist Economy**

**Scott Cohen Commandment:** "The internet did not kill the record label. It killed the lazy, opaque, extractive label. The future belongs to the label that behaves like a platform: open, transparent, and endlessly configurable to the needs of the artist."

## Darren Hemmings: Algorithmic Growth

**Core Philosophy:** Algorithms are just fan behavior, codified. Build for the fan, and the algorithm will follow.

**The Hemmings Doctrine:** The algorithm is the most powerful A&R and radio programmer in history, and it works for free if you know how to feed it.

### Strategy Framework

1. **The Algorithmic Trinity** (Release Radar, Discover Weekly, Radio and Autoplay)
2. **Metadata as a Marketing Superpower**
3. **The "Trigger City" and Geospatial Algorithmic Strategy**
4. **The Modern Waterfall Release Strategy**
5. **Data-Driven Audience Development**
6. **The Paid Media Efficiency Engine**
7. **Editorial Playlists as a Byproduct, Not a Goal**
8. **The Streaming Narrative**
9. **The Measurement Framework**
10. **The Label Transformation**

**Darren Hemmings Commandment:** "The algorithm is not your enemy. It is a mirror reflecting what your audience genuinely feels about your music."

## IOTN Records Implementation

### Immediate Actions
1. Audit Current Streaming Strategy
2. Establish Data Foundation
3. Launch Pilot Programs

### Organizational Changes
- New Roles: Chief Platform Officer, Chief Data Officer, Artist Success Team leads
- Process Redesign: Cross-functional artist squads, weekly Data Room reviews

### Technology Stack Requirements
- Core Platform: Global distribution engine, real-time data lake, open API
- Marketing Tools: A/B testing, lookalike audience generation, Trigger City detection
- Commerce Infrastructure: Direct-to-fan store, token-gated access, seamless ticketing

### Success Metrics
- Cohen Metrics: Artist Business Health Scorecard, portfolio diversification
- Hemmings Metrics: Save-to-Listener Ratio, Completion Rate, Listener-to-Follower Conversion

### Long-Term Vision
Build IOTN Records as the world's first fully algorithmically-native, platform-based label.
